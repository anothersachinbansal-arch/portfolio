## Quick Canva Steps:
1. Go to Canva.com
2. Search "Facebook Cover" or "Social Media Post"
3. Use 1200×630px template
4. Upload your photo
5. Add professional text
6. Download as JPG (high quality)
7. Save as `og-image.jpg` in public folder
